# Evidence

**Evidence** is the **ESG product evaluation** tool designed **for and by consumers**.

## Features

- **ESG product evaluation** — transparent scoring based on environmental, social, and governance criteria
- **Mobile application** — simplified access in-store or online
- **Website** — search, comparison, and community contributions
- **Open data** — evaluations enrich the Coherence database

## Target audience

Evidence serves **consumers** who want to make informed purchasing decisions and contribute to a more sustainable economy.

## How it works

1. **Search** for a product via the app or website
2. **Review** its ESG score and criteria details
3. **Contribute** by evaluating or enriching product data

!!! tip "Co-creation"
    Evidence relies on active consumer participation. Your contribution improves evaluation quality for everyone.
