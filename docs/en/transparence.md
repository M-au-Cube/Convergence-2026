# Transparency

**Transparency** is Convergence's ESG platform for **companies** and **public organizations**.

## Features

- **ESG dashboards** — real-time visualization of environmental, social, and governance indicators
- **Sustainability report production** — generation of reports aligned with ESG standards
- **Sustainable decision suggestions** — recommendations based on your data analysis
- **Collaboration proposals** — connecting with other stakeholders on shared ESG topics

## Target audience

| Stakeholder | Use case |
|-------------|----------|
| Companies | ESG management, reporting, continuous improvement |
| Public organizations | Citizen transparency, sustainable policies |

## Access

The Transparency platform will be available through a dedicated website. Login information will be shared with partners at deployment.
