# Coherence

**Coherence** is Convergence ESG's **central database**. It structures and unifies all data collected by our four products.

## Role

Coherence ensures **consistency** of ESG analyses through:

- A **shared data model** across all products
- **Quality and traceability standards** for information
- **APIs** enabling data exchange between stakeholders (respecting confidentiality rules)

## Data sources

| Product | Data type |
|---------|-----------|
| Transparency | ESG indicators for companies & public organizations |
| Resilience | Questionnaire and audit results |
| Evidence | Consumer product evaluations |

## Principles

- **Interoperability** — open formats and recognized standards
- **Traceability** — every data point is attributed to its source
- **Confidentiality** — aggregation and anonymization per charter rules

!!! info "Shared foundation"
    Coherence links stakeholders across scales: it helps companies understand consumer expectations, and consumers benefit from organizational transparency.
