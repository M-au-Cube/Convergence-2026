# Resilience

**Resilience** provides **ESG questionnaires and audits** for companies seeking to assess and strengthen their sustainable practices.

## Features

- **Structured ESG questionnaires** — assessment by domain (environment, social, governance)
- **In-depth audits** — detailed analysis of processes and impacts
- **Scores and benchmarks** — comparison with industry standards
- **Action plans** — prioritized recommendations to improve ESG resilience

## Target audience

Resilience primarily serves **companies** of all sizes, from SMEs to large corporations.

## Integration with Coherence

Questionnaire and audit results feed the **Coherence** database, enabling aggregated analyses while respecting individual data confidentiality.
